Concept Summary

TGSM organizes systemic movement into structural energy layers and persistence signals, showing where risk concentrates, when stability is genuine, and how patterns evolve over time. It interprets directional energy and regime shifts as changes in risk position and momentum—whether accelerating, reducing, or holding steady. This clarity means trustable signals, explainable decisions, and confidence that scales across conditions and time horizons.
